Jokgu App Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, Phone-Number, D.O.B. and other data you provide when creating an account.
  • Club participation data: Attendance, Game sign‑ups, and membership dues status
  • Usage Data: App interactions, device information, and crash reports.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Jokgu App features.
  • Send notifications, updates, and support responses.
  • Analyze usage to enhance performance and security.

3. Third‑party services

The App has options to open external services such as Venmo for payments, Kakao for joining our association's group chat, and YouTube for related content. When you follow these links, their own privacy policies govern.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With service providers who help operate our app (analytics, cloud hosting, customer support).
  • If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • In aggregated or anonymized form for research and product improvement.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

Jokgu App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
